Chex Muddy Buddies

Recipe by Chex Brand on the Allrecipes Website
A fun, sweet & crunchy snack for chocolate and peanut butter lovers.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups Rice Chex ® cereal
  • 3 cups Corn Chex ® cereal
  • 3 cups Chocolate Chex ® cereal
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup butter or margarine
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar

Directions:

  1. Combine all cereals in a large bowl; set aside.
  2. Add together: chocolate chips, peanut butter and butter to at least a 1-quart microwaveable bowl. Microwave, uncovered, for at least 1 minute, then stir til combined. Continue warming mixture as stirring as needed. Stir in vanilla.
  3. Pour the chocolate mix over the combined cereal and stir until coated. Transfer entire mixture to a large resealable plastic bag.
  4. Add the powdered sugar to the mix and seal. Shake until coated. Spread out the mixture onto wax or parchment paper to cool.

I grew up having this treat around the holidays. It's really yummy and great for anyone who loves sweet things with peanut butter and chocolate!
